# Multiplier

A multiplication facts practice game. Play at **[multiplier.ykj.works](https://multiplier.ykj.works)**.

## How to play

1. Set a time limit (or switch to Stopwatch mode in Options)
2. Click **Start**
3. A multiplication question appears — type your answer and press **Enter** or tap **Submit**
4. The answer is revealed; press **Enter** or tap **Continue** to move on
5. Click **Stop** or press **Esc** at any time to see your results

## Features

- **Countdown mode** — race against a timer (1–10 minutes)
- **Stopwatch mode** — practice at your own pace
- **Hide timer** — remove the distraction of watching the clock
- **Result grid** — after the game, see every fact you attempted colored green (correct) or red (wrong), with a Show All Facts option
- **Browse mode** — when the game isn't running, click any cell to highlight its row and column, click row/column labels to highlight just that row or column, or click the corner cells to highlight the main or anti-diagonal

## Keyboard shortcuts

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Enter | Submit answer / Continue |
| Esc | Stop game |

## Tech

Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no build step, no dependencies.
