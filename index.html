Stopwatch
How to play:
  1. Set a time limit or switch to Stopwatch mode in Options.
  2. Click or tap Start.
  3. A multiplication question will appear — type your answer and press Enter or tap Submit.
  4. The answer will be revealed; press Enter or tap Continue to proceed.
  5. Click or tap Stop or press Esc at any time to see how you did.
